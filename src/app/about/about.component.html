About Swagbacker

Swagbacker was born out of a job interview task. Since then we have gone from strength to strength thanks to the support of the community!

How it Works

Every week we will put up a product to be backed. If enough orders are placed to reach the product's goal, we'll get them shipped out to everybody who ordered.

Once the backing period is over, if the product was funded, we will send out an email to all backers asking for their delivery address and any additional details (size/colour). New orders will not be able to be placed after the backing period.
